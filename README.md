# Online Retail RFM Customer Segmentation

## Business Context
An online UK-based retailer wanted to better understand customer behavior 
and identify high-value segments to improve retention and marketing strategy.

## Objective
Segment 5,800+ customers using RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) analysis 
and K-Means clustering to enable targeted customer engagement.

## Tools
Python | Pandas | Scikit-learn | Matplotlib | Google Colab

## Key Findings
- **65% of customers** are active but low-spend — highest opportunity for upsell
- **Only 27 customers** drive the majority of revenue (High Value + VIP segments)
- **34% of customers** have not purchased in 400+ days — at risk of permanent churn
- VIP segment (wholesale buyers) averages $547K in spend — requires dedicated account management
